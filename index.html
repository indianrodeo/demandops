Common Questions

What are the messaging frameworks that I use?

Why, How, What, and Why Now. These frameworks help clarify your value proposition, differentiation, and urgency. Essential for cutting through noise in competitive markets.

How much of my time do you want to ramp up?

5 hours of dedicated founder time per month. Occasionally, I may ask for more during critical phases like campaign launches or strategic pivots.

How is your "way of working"?

Very hands-on and always on keyboard. I swim in and out of your CRMs, sales transcripts, documentation, and sync with your team in real-time meetings.

How do you propose campaign strategy?

I build a thesis based on structured deep dives into your market, competitors, and customer conversations. Then present the strategy to founders before execution.

What channels are you good at?

My primary strengths are SEO, Cold Email, and founder LinkedIn content. I have working knowledge of ad platforms and solve point-use cases for any channel. I rely on LLMs to accelerate execution.

What metric do you hold yourself accountable for?

Qualified pipeline. I focus on generating demos and opportunities that match your ICP, not vanity metrics.

Can you build automations for my RevOps cadence?

Yes, I use CRM and n8n automations for revenue ops cadences. From lead routing to follow-up sequences and pipeline alerts.
